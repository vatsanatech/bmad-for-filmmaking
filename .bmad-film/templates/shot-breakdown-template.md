## Example Usage:

### SHOT 1-1: Establishing Shot of Police Station

**Shot Number**: 1-1
**Shot Type**: Wide Shot
**Duration**: 4 seconds

#### Camera Specifications
- **Lens**: 24mm (wide angle)
- **Camera Angle**: Eye-level, slight low angle (looking up at building)
- **Camera Height**: 5 feet (eye level)
- **Camera Movement**: Static (locked off)
- **Focus**: Deep focus (everything sharp)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
- **Frame Rate**: 24fps

#### Composition & Framing
- **Framing**: Building centered in frame, filling 60% of frame, sky visible above
- **Screen Direction**: N/A (static establishing)
- **Foreground Elements**: Street with few passing vehicles (blurred)
- **Background Elements**: Sky, adjacent buildings
- **Depth Layers**: Street (foreground), police station (mid-ground), sky (background)

#### Lighting
- **Lighting Type**: Natural light
- **Time of Day**: Pre-dawn (5:30 AM, dark but lightening)
- **Light Direction**: Ambient (no direct sun yet)
- **Light Quality**: Soft, diffused
- **Color Temperature**: Cool (blue hour)
- **Mood**: Somber, institutional, waiting for dawn
- **Practical Lights**: Station lights on inside (warm glow from windows)

#### Action & Blocking
- **Character(s) in Shot**: None (establishing)
- **Action Description**: Static shot of police station exterior, 2-3 vehicles passing by
- **Performance Notes**: N/A
- **Continuity Notes**: Building number visible, signage clear

#### Sound
- **Dialogue**: None
- **Sound Effects**: Distant traffic, early morning city ambient
- **Ambient Sound**: Birds starting to chirp (dawn approaching)
- **Music**: None (or very subtle atmospheric score beginning)
- **Silence**: Relative quiet (city not yet awake)

#### Emotional & Storytelling
- **Emotional Objective**: Establish institutional authority, early morning isolation
- **Story Purpose**: Introduce primary location, set tone (official, serious)
- **Visual Subtext**: Police station as fortress/barrier, lights on inside (work never stops)
- **Genre Elements**: Thriller/Suspense - institutional setting, cool colors, early hour creates unease
- **Pacing Note**: Hold for 4 seconds (let audience orient)

#### Technical Notes
- **Special Equipment**: Tripod
- **VFX Required**: None
- **Color Grading**: Enhance blue hour cool tones, slightly desaturate
- **Complexity Level**: Simple
- **Prep Time Estimate**: 5 minutes (tripod setup, frame building)
- **Shooting Time Estimate**: 2 minutes (2-3 takes for passing vehicles)
- **Takes Estimated**: 3

#### Coverage & Editing
- **Edit Priority**: Must-have (establishing shot)
- **Previous Shot**: None (opening shot of scene)
- **Next Shot**: 1-2 (Interior police station, close-up of clock)
- **Match Requirements**: N/A
- **Alternative Angles**: None planned
- **Cutaway Opportunities**: None
