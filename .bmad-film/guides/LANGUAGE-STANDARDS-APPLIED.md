## Key Principles Applied to ALL Agents

### 1. DEFAULT = Simple Bollywood Hindi
- Not literary/formal Hindi
- Not too much English
- Natural code-switching
- Mass-market accessible

### 2. Dialect Support
- User can specify: Haryanvi, Punjabi, Gujarati, Rajasthani, Bengali, Marathi, etc.
- Agent will adapt language accordingly
- Maintain accessibility even with dialect

### 3. Emotional Scenes = More Hindi
- Love confessions: Hindi
- Dramatic moments: Hindi
- Cultural contexts: Hindi
- Modern/tech: English OK
