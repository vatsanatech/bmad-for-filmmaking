## FRAMEWORK DESCRIPTIONS (for user when they want more detail)

### 1. Save the Cat 15 Beats (Blake Snyder)
Hollywood ka most-used formula â€” 15 beats ke exact page numbers hain (Opening Image pg.1, Catalyst pg.12, Midpoint pg.55, All Is Lost pg.75, Final Image pg.110). Har beat ki emotional function clearly defined hai. Bollywood ke liye page ratios adjust karne padte hain lekin structure same rehta hai. Emotional transformation ki clearest roadmap.
**Questions shape**: Page number ratios, "All Is Lost" moment, Opening/Final Image mirror.
**Output**: 15 beats clearly labeled with page counts + emotional function.

### 2. Hero's Journey 12 Stages (Campbell/Vogler)
Joseph Campbell aur Christopher Vogler ka mythic structure â€” 12 stages mein universal human transformation mapped hai. Ordinary World se lekar Return with the Elixir tak ka safar. Psychologically deep, spiritually resonant. Har stage ka ek symbolic meaning hota hai protagonist ki inner journey ke liye. Indian epics (Ramayana, Mahabharata) bhi isi structure mein hain.
**Questions shape**: Mentor figure, threshold moment, ordeal design, elixir/transformation.
**Output**: 12 stages mapped with mythic function + character arc per stage.

### 3. Three-Act Beat Map (Classical)
Sabse foundational structure â€” Act 1 (Setup, ~25%), Act 2 (Confrontation, ~50%), Act 3 (Resolution, ~25%). Key beats: Inciting Incident, Plot Turn 1, Midpoint, Plot Turn 2, Climax, Resolution. Almost every film at heart follows this, even if surface structure differs. Solid starting point for any story.
**Questions shape**: Turning point design, midpoint stakes escalation, climax approach.
**Output**: Three-act map with key beats per act + page distribution.

### 4. Dan Harmon Story Circle (8 Points)
TV writers ka favourite â€” 8 points ka simple circular arc. Protagonist apni "zone of comfort" se nikalta hai, kuch chahta hai, unfamiliar situation mein jaata hai, adapt karta hai, paata hai, price chukaaata hai, wapas aata hai â€” lekin changed hokar. Community aur Rick & Morty ka DNA. Cyclical nature short films ke liye perfect.
**Questions shape**: What protagonist wants, unfamiliar situation, price paid.
**Output**: 8-point circle with character state at each point.

### 5. Bollywood Interval Structure
Indian cinema ka unique DNA â€” pre-interval mein story establish hoti hai, interval ek strong emotional/plot moment pe aata hai (audience intermission mein baat karte rahein), second half mein escalation aur resolution hoti hai. Songs structural beats ki tarah kaam karte hain â€” Song 1 (character world), Song 2 (romance), Song 3 (montage/time), Song 4 (lament/climax approach).
**Questions shape**: Interval scene design, song placement moments (3-6 songs), climax type.
**Output**: Pre-interval + interval + post-interval beats + song moments clearly marked.

### 6. Seven-Point Structure (Larry Brooks)
Larry Brooks ka structure â€” 4 parts + 3 turning points. Hook mein audience pakad, Plot Turn 1 mein conflict introduce, Midpoint/Pinch mein stakes raise, Plot Turn 2 mein final push, Resolution mein closure. Escalating tension ka master framework. Action-thriller ke liye tailored.
**Questions shape**: Two pinch points, protagonist's response phase, attack phase.
**Output**: 7 points with dramatic function + escalation level per point.

### 7. Sequence Approach (8 Sequences)
Screenplay ko 8 mini-movies mein todna â€” har sequence ka apna beginning-middle-end hota hai, apna dramatic question hota hai, apna protagonist goal hota hai. Act 2 ka "sagging middle" problem naturally solve hota hai kyunki har sequence mein local stakes hain. Feature films ke liye pacing ka most practical tool.
**Questions shape**: Local goal per sequence, sequence-specific dramatic question.
**Output**: 8 sequence breakdown with individual mini-arc per sequence.

### 8. Fichtean Curve
Setup nahi â€” seedha crisis se shuru karo. Multiple crises ek ke baad ek, continuously escalating. Har crisis protagonist ko todta hai aur phir woh uthta hai. Climax sabse badi crisis hoti hai. Exposition minimal ya no exposure â€” audience discovers world through crises. Adrenaline-first storytelling.
**Questions shape**: Number of crises, crisis escalation order, starting crisis design.
**Output**: Crisis map with escalation arc, each crisis higher than last.

### 9. Story Grid Obligatory Scenes (Shawn Coyne)
Shawn Coyne ka editorial framework â€” har genre ke kuch "obligatory scenes" hain jo audience expect karta hai. Romance mein commitment scene mandatory hai. Thriller mein false accusation. Horror mein monster reveal. Yeh scenes skip karne se audience unsatisfied feel karta hai chahe baaki sab perfect ho.
**Questions shape**: Genre obligatory scenes checklist, conventions check, what can't be missed.
**Output**: Genre-specific obligatory scenes list + where they fall in story.

### 10. Emotional Beat Mapping
Plot beats nahi â€” emotional beats map karo. Protagonist ki har major emotion ko track karo (anger â†’ guilt â†’ denial â†’ acceptance). Yeh emotional spine hi screenplay ki actual journey hoti hai. Events sirf emotional shifts ke triggers hote hain â€” beats are FEELINGS, not scenes.
**Questions shape**: Emotional starting state, each emotional shift, emotional destination.
**Output**: Emotional arc visualization â€” feeling at each story point, not just events.

### 11. KishÅtenketsu (4 Parts)
Japanese/Korean structure â€” conflict nahi, twist ke through drama. Ki (Introduction) â†’ ShÅ (Development) â†’ Ten (Unexpected Twist/Change) â†’ Ketsu (Reconciliation). Drama arise karta hai unexpected turn se, confrontation se nahi. Western audience ko surprising â€” conflict-free storytelling jo phir bhi engaging ho.
**Questions shape**: What's the "Ten" (twist), how does it reconcile with the opening.
**Output**: 4-part structure with twist design + reconciliation logic.

### 12. Bollywood Masala Beat
Indian commercial cinema ka entertainment rhythm â€” har 15-20 minutes mein emotional register badalta hai (comedy â†’ romance â†’ drama â†’ action â†’ emotional scene â†’ song â†’ action). Audience ko ek hi feeling mein nahi rehne deta. Interval ke baad energy reset hoti hai. Full entertainment package.
**Questions shape**: Entertainment register rotation plan, song trigger moments, interval position.
**Output**: Rotation chart of comedy/romance/drama/action/song beats + interval position.
